## Week 1: Identity & Access (The "Entra ID" Phase)
*Leverages: JH Kelly AD/Okta experience.*
- **Focus:** Domain management, Entra Connect (Hybrid sync), and Conditional Access.
- **Resources:** [MS Learn: Deploy and manage a Microsoft 365 tenant](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/training/paths/m365-admin-tenant-manage/).
- **Action Task:** Set up a free [M365 Developer Sandbox](https://developer.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-365/dev-program). Sync a mock local AD to Entra ID.
- **Study Goal:** 10 Hours.

## Week 2: Endpoint Management (The "Intune" Phase)
*Leverages: Daimler iOS/Windows fleet expertise.*
- **Focus:** Device enrollment (Autopilot/ADE), Configuration Profiles, and Compliance Policies.
- **Resources:** [MS Learn: Manage endpoints in Microsoft 365](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/training/paths/m365-admin-endpoint-manage/).
- **Action Task:** Build one "Zero-Trust" compliance policy that requires MFA and a specific OS version for iOS devices.
- **Study Goal:** 12 Hours.

## Week 3: Security & Compliance (The "Defender" Phase)
*Leverages: Cisco Cybersecurity Essentials cert.*
- **Focus:** Microsoft Defender for Endpoint, DLP (Data Loss Prevention), and eDiscovery.
- **Resources:** [MS Learn: Manage security and compliance](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/training/paths/m365-admin-security-compliance-manage/).
- **Action Task:** Run a "Security Assessment" in your sandbox and resolve 3 high-priority recommendations.
- **Study Goal:** 12 Hours.

## Week 4: Review & Exam (The "Certification" Phase)
- **Focus:** Practice exams and gap filling.
- **Tools:** Use [MeasureUp](https://www.measureup.com/) or [Tutorials Dojo](https://tutorialsdojo.com/) for high-quality practice sets.
- **Action Task:** Take 3 practice exams. Aim for >85% consistency.
- **Final Act:** Schedule exam for Friday/Saturday of Week 4.
