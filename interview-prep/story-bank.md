## Story 1: Enterprise Meraki SD-WAN Transformation
**Theme:** Infrastructure Architecture, Project Management, High Availability
**Primary Target:** **KDInfotech** (Consultative/Technical Lead)

### [S] Situation
At JH Kelly, our network infrastructure across multiple industrial and office sites was inconsistent. Legacy hardware made security enforcement slow and field operations were prone to connectivity drops.

### [T] Task
I was tasked with leading the design and implementation of an enterprise-wide SD-WAN solution to unify the network under a single pane of glass and ensure high availability for mission-critical apps.

### [A] Action
- **Site Discovery:** Audited all physical sites for power/cabling constraints.
- **Architectural Design:** Architected a Meraki-based topology with dual-WAN failover.
- **Deployment:** Lead physical installation and virtual configuration of MX security appliances and switches.
- **Security:** Standardized firewall rules and Content Filtering across all sites.

### [R] Result
Resulted in a **100% reduction in major site outages** and a 60% reduction in management overhead through centralized monitoring.

### [+R] Relevance
"This demonstrates my ability to act as a **Technical Lead** who can design high-level strategy and execute ground-level implementation. At **KDInfotech**, I can apply this same rigorous approach to your clients' most complex multi-site infrastructure challenges."

## Story 4: Critical Infrastructure High Availability
**Theme:** Virtualization, SAN/NAS, 24/7 Operations
**Primary Target:** **CorSource** (Critical Infrastructure)

### [S] Situation
Our primary virtualization cluster at JH Kelly was approaching end-of-life, and the underlying SAN storage was reaching capacity, risking performance bottlenecks for critical 24/7 industrial systems.

### [T] Task
I lead the technical migration of our production environment to a new high-availability hardware stack (VMware/SAN) with zero impact on active business operations.

### [A] Action
- **Storage Management:** Performed SAN hot-swaps and managed high-capacity iSCSI/RAID configurations.
- **Migration:** Executed a tiered migration of VMs using vMotion, prioritizing critical databases and industrial control systems.
- **Monitoring:** Implemented proactive alerting for power (UPS) and hardware health to prevent silent failures.

### [R] Result
Increased storage throughput by **35%** and successfully moved the entire environment with **zero unplanned downtime** for end-users.

### [+R] Relevance
"CorSource focuses on **24/7 critical infrastructure**. This story demonstrates my technical maturity in managing the physical and virtual 'heavy lifting' required to keep high-stakes operations running smoothly around the clock."
